The Kryn Dynasty

In the shadowed expanses of Xhorhas, where ancient wastes stretch under eternal skies, rises the mighty Kryn Dynasty. Founded by the visionary drow queen Leylas Kryn—the eternal Bright Queen—she led her followers from the oppressive depths of the Underdark, breaking free from the venomous grasp of Lolth the Spider Queen. Now, this thriving realm pulses with diversity: proud drow stand alongside fierce orcs, cunning goblinoids, enigmatic tieflings, and resilient humans, all united as equals. Nomadic clans roam the wilds, chasing the thunderous herds of Xhorhasian megafauna, their hunts echoing tales of survival and glory.

The Settlement of Jigow

Your epic saga ignites in Jigow, a vibrant coastal haven woven from a tapestry of villages. Ruled by two wise elders—a shrewd goblin and a stalwart orc—this lively community thrives on spirited rivalries and unbreakable bonds. The Kryn Dynasty's vigilant guardians, the Aurora Watch, stand sentinel here, commanded by the formidable Taskhand Durth Mirimm, ensuring the fragile peace amid the whispers of the wastes.

"The Dynasty stands united under the light of the Luxon."
