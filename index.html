Call of the Netherdeep

An Orientation Guide for Adventurers

The Kryn Dynasty

In the shadowed expanses of Xhorhas, where ancient wastes stretch under eternal skies, rises the mighty Kryn Dynasty. Founded by the visionary drow queen Leylas Kryn—the eternal Bright Queen—she led her followers from the oppressive depths of the Underdark, breaking free from the venomous grasp of Lolth the Spider Queen. Now, this thriving realm pulses with diversity: proud drow stand alongside fierce orcs, cunning goblinoids, enigmatic tieflings, and resilient humans, all united as equals. Nomadic clans roam the wilds, chasing the thunderous herds of Xhorhasian megafauna, their hunts echoing tales of survival and glory.

The Settlement of Jigow

Your epic saga ignites in Jigow, a vibrant coastal haven woven from a tapestry of villages. Ruled by two wise elders—a shrewd goblin and a stalwart orc—this lively community thrives on spirited rivalries and unbreakable bonds. The Kryn Dynasty's vigilant guardians, the Aurora Watch, stand sentinel here, commanded by the formidable Taskhand Durth Mirimm, ensuring the fragile peace amid the whispers of the wastes.

"The Dynasty stands united under the light of the Luxon."

The Festival of Merit

In the heart of Jigow, where the air crackles with anticipation and the spirit of competition burns bright, you've arrived just in time for the legendary Festival of Merit. Here, the townsfolk's unyielding drive for excellence transforms everyday rivalries into exhilarating spectacles. You'll share the arena with a band of daring local heroes-in-the-making, each one a force of nature ready to test their mettle against yours.

Ayo Jabe (Water Genasi): A fierce and unyielding hunter, born of storm-tossed seas, she leads with raw passion, shielding her allies like a tidal wave crashing against the shore.
Dermot Wurder (Goblin): A serene cleric whose quiet wisdom anchors his companions through the fiercest storms, prioritizing their well-being above all else.
Galsariad Ardyth (Drow): A scholarly wizard from the depths, his sharp intellect and fascination with ancient lore are laced with a wry, enigmatic humor that disarms as much as it delights.
Irvan Wastewalker (Human): A wandering nomad forged by the unforgiving wastes, his easygoing charm belies a deadly precision with the blade, honed through endless trials.
Maggie Keeneyes (Ogre): A colossal tactician with a poet's soul, her strategic brilliance and unshakeable resolve make her the unbreakable foundation of her crew.
"In Jigow, competition is the language of friendship."

The Luxon & Consecution

At the core of the Dynasty's faith shines the Luxon, a radiant entity of eternal light and profound rebirth. Through the sacred rite of Consecution, devoted souls are forged anew, accumulating wisdom across lifetimes in a cycle of endless enlightenment.

Rebirth: Should a consecuted soul perish within the sacred radius of a Luxon beacon—100 miles—their essence is captured and reborn in a newborn form nearby, carrying forward the echoes of past lives.

Character Questions

As you forge your hero's path, ponder these sparks of introspection to breathe life into their legend:

  • What's one small heroic deed you've already accomplished? Recall a moment where your courage changed the tide, no matter how humble.
  • Do you give your trust easily, or do people have to earn it? Reflect on the bonds that define you—forged in fire or given freely?
  • What do you want more than anything else? How will adventuring help? Unveil your deepest desire and the perilous roads that lead to its fulfillment.

Setting Your Goals

Envision the flames that propel your character through peril and triumph. Craft ambitions that span the horizons of time:

  • Short-term goals beckon like treasures within reach—perhaps amassing gold for gleaming armor, claiming victory in the Festival of Merit, or forging an alliance with a rival that sparks unexpected kinship.
  • Medium-term goals demand grit and cunning—carve your name into Jigow's lore, unravel the veils of your enigmatic past, or master arcane arts that bend reality to your will.
  • Long-term goals soar like legends untold—ascend as an immortal hero, pierce the mysteries of the Luxon, vanquish shadows that threaten the world, or reshape the very destiny of the Dynasty.
No need to etch these in stone—let them evolve as your tale unfolds, twisting with every choice and revelation!
Character Tip: The wastes whisper dangers untold. Hone your Athletics and Survival skills—they'll be your lifeline in the trials ahead.
"The Festival of Merit awaits. Answer the call."

What You Know: Echoes of the World

As heirs to Exandria's vast tapestry, your characters enter this saga armed with ancient truths. Share these whispers with your allies as you weave your backstories:

Kryn Dynasty: This beacon of power in Xhorhas was born from rebellion. Queen Leylas Kryn, the Bright Queen, guided her drow followers from the Underdark's shadows, escaping Lolth's cruel web. Today, it's a mosaic of souls—drow, orcs, goblinoids, tieflings, humans—living as one. Nomads wander the wilds in clans, pursuing colossal beasts that roam the land.

Jigow: Your journey sparks in this coastal mosaic of villages, home to folk from across Xhorhas. Founded by goblin and orc clans, it's steered by two elders—one goblin, one orc. The Aurora Watch, the Dynasty's iron fist, watches over all, led by the drow Taskhand Durth Mirimm. Here, competition fuels the spirit, with friendly feuds igniting in the central hub known as the Jumble.

The Luxon: The Dynasty's guiding light, this deity of rebirth bestows secrets upon the faithful. Through consecution, souls transcend death, reborn with accumulated wisdom across forms—from drow to goblin, bugbear to orc—unlocking deeper truths of existence. (This rite offers no mechanical edge but enriches your character's lore.)

Rebirth's Embrace: A consecuted soul felled within 100 miles of a Luxon beacon is reborn in a newborn Humanoid nearby, carrying the flame of past lives forward.
"Embrace the light, for in rebirth lies eternity."
